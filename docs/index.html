Guide to the Business Profile plugin for WordPress

This guide will help you install and configure the plugin for your site.

Further support for this plugin can be found at themeofthecrop.com.

Installation

The following steps describe how to upload the Business Profile plugin to an existing WordPress installation. If you have not yet installed WordPress, consult the WordPress documentation for more information.

  1. Unpack the business-profile.zip file. It should create a folder named /business-profile/.
  2. Upload the /business-profile/ folder to your WordPress plugin directory at /wp-content/plugins/.
  3. Go to your WordPress admin dashboard at yoursite.com/wp-admin and click the Plugins item in the menu on the left.
  4. Find Business Profile in the list of Plugins and click the Activate link below the plugin name.

The plugin is now active. Next you'll need to get it setup.

Quick Setup

To get this plugin working as quickly as possible, all you need to do is fill out your business details and add the widget

  1. Go to the Business Profile page in your admin dashboard. It should appear near the bottom of the menu on the left.
  2. Fill out your business details on this page and click the Save button at the bottom.
  3. Simply paste [contact-card] into the content of any page or post. Or add the new Contact Card widget to any sidebar.

Once you've done that, your users can view your business details. Read the Shortcode section to learn about customizing what details are displayed.

Shortcode

Prints the contact details to your site. All details will be displayed with the default shortcode [contact-card]. To hide any of the components, set the attribute to 0.

[contact-card show_name=1 show_address=1 show_get_directions=1 show_phone=1 show_contact=1 show_opening_hours=1 show_opening_hours_brief=0 show_map=1]

  • show_name
    Whether or not to show the name of the business.
  • show_address
    Whether or not to show the address.
  • show_get_directions
    Whether or not to show a link to Google Maps with directions from the user's current location to your address.
  • show_phone
    Whether or not to show your phone number.
  • show_cellphone
    Whether or not to show your cellphone number.
  • show_faxphone
    Whether or not to show your fax number.
  • show_contact
    Whether or not to show a link to your contact page if set. Otherwise it will show the email address if one has been added to the Business Profile settings page.
  • show_opening_hours
    Whether or not to show your opening hours.
  • show_opening_hours_brief
    Whether or not to show a short, one-line version of your opening hours. This is turned off by default. Set it to 1 to turn it on.
  • show_map
    Whether or not to show a Google Map with your location.
  • show_booking_link
    Whether or not to show the link to Restaurant Reservations page if you use that plugin too.
